Know Better Cloud - a creation by James Radtke, a Senior Solutions Architect at AWS.(*) This landing zone was created as a central place for a number of my online projects.

One of my goals is to cover some cloud fundamentals and related topics (like how to manage SSH keys, or what services have releases or updates that are worth mentioning).
What I do *not* intend to do is create another cloud course, or how-to content. There are tons of great resources out there for that already. This is more a collection of my anecdotes, or myself walking through demos.
